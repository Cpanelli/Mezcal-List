Christopher's Mezcal List

Del Maguey Chichicapa

  • Maguey: Espadín
  • Style: Joven
  • Origin: Oaxaca, Mexico
  • ABV: Varies (typically around 47%)
  • Distillation: Artisanal, small-batch distillation
  • Aroma: Herbaceous, citrus, light smoke
  • Palate: Smooth, fruity, notes of tropical fruits and roasted agave
  • Appearance: Clear, medium body
  • How to Enjoy: In a mezcal margherita

Crema de Mezcal

  • Maguey: Espadín
  • Style: Cream Liqueur
  • Origin: Oaxaca, Mexico
  • ABV: 16%
  • Distillation: Artisanal, small-batch distillation
  • Aroma: Sweet, smoky, hints of chocolate and coffee
  • Palate: Rich, creamy, balanced sweetness with smoky undertones
  • Appearance: Creamy texture with a light caramel color
  • How to Enjoy: In a Negroni or a Mezcal coffee martini

Del Maguey Minero

  • Maguey: Espadín
  • Style: Joven
  • Origin: Oaxaca, Mexico
  • ABV: Varies (typically around 49%)
  • Distillation: Artisanal, small-batch distillation
  • Aroma: Sweet, fruity, earthy, smoky
  • Palate: Smooth, complex, notes of tropical fruits, roasted agave, and chocolate
  • Appearance: Clear, medium body
  • How to Enjoy: Old Fashioned
